Our Rich History

Maringa was founded in 1947, strategically planned with wide avenues and numerous green spaces, earning it the nickname "Garden City." Its development was driven by coffee cultivation and diversified agriculture.

Key historical milestones include the construction of the iconic Cathedral of Maringa, a symbol of the city's growth, and its recognition as a leading urban center in Northern Parana.

Read more about the pioneers who shaped Maringa's early days and its transformation into a modern city.
