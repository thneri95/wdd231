How to Use This Tool

Mastering verbs is crucial for fluency. Use this tool to explore and practice the most common Spanish verbs. Simply use the filter to narrow your search by verb ending (-ar, -er, -ir), or browse the full list. Click on any verb to see its complete present-tense conjugation table.
