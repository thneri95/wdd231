Site Plan: Spanish Learning Hub

Site Name

Spanish Learning Hub

Reason

I chose this subject because I was a Spanish teacher at Wizard School ( a company of Elder Carlos Martins in Brazil) and I am passionate about creating useful tools that make language learning more accessible. This project is a perfect opportunity to combine my teaching background with the web development skills we are learning.

Site Purpose

The Spanish Learning Hub will provide essential tools and resources for beginner to intermediate Spanish learners. The site will offer a daily vocabulary word, an interactive verb conjugator tool, and a resource page with a newsletter signup form. The overall goal is to provide a practical, high-quality resource that makes learning Spanish more accessible and engaging.

Scenarios

  • "I'm learning Spanish verbs but always forget the conjugations. Where can I quickly look up how to conjugate a verb like 'hablar'?"
  • "How can I easily learn one new Spanish word every day?"
  • "I'm looking for some general tips to improve my Spanish learning. Is there a resources section?"
