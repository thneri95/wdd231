    Cultural Tips

    Understanding culture is key to mastering any language. Here are some quick tips:

    • Formal vs Informal: Use usted for formal situations and tú for friends and family.
    • Gestures: The “OK” sign can have different meanings — always pay attention to context.
    • Regional Differences: Vocabulary varies between Spain, Mexico, and Latin America — embrace the diversity!
